Our Curriculum

What is the Edexcel IGCSE Curriculum?

The Edexcel International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) is a globally recognized qualification for students typically aged 14 to 16. Designed to be both accessible and challenging, it emphasizes academic excellence while allowing flexibility through a wide range of subject choices.

Core Subjects

STEM

Our curriculum includes the complete list of STEM subjects, which includes Physics, Biology, Chemistry and IT

Social Studies

Subjects included in this field are History and Sociology.

Business and Economics

For aspiring entrepreneurs, subjects in this category for students to choose include Business, Economics and Accounting.

Language Literature

Students may also take language subjects as their core subjects. These include Mandarin and German. These are useful for those aiming for overseas universities, especially those in Germany who provide a full scholarship for a certain level in German comprehension.

Elective Classes

Basketball

One of the most popular sport elective among students. Those who join will also have the option to be a part of the Basketball School Team should they pass the selection process

Badminton

Also another popular sport among our students. Just like basketball, students will also have a chance to join the Badminton School Team and participate in inter-school competitions.

Music Ensemble

Students can group into pairs, trios or groups of 4-5 and unleash their musical talents for the entire school to see. Members will participate in end-of-year performances and even collaborate with Performing Arts to provide music for drama performances!

Performing Arts

For those who adore acting and wish to express themselves, performing arts will allow you to do just that. Join other students and create a drama an unforgetable drama performance!

Students are assessed through a combination of written exams and practical coursework depending on the subject. Exams are externally marked by Edexcel examiners to ensure consistency and fairness.

We provide after-school tutoring, exam prep workshops, and access to online learning platforms to help students succeed in their IGCSE subjects.

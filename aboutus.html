About Us

Santa Laurensia Senior High School provides holistic education combining academic excellence with character formation. Our bilingual environment nurtures globally aware students with strong moral values.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading educational institution that nurtures students into joyful, responsible, and globally aware individuals with a strong foundation of moral integrity.

Our Mission

  • To provide a holistic education system with a global perspective
  • To establish a school community that develops good character and the ability to interact socially in appropriate ways
  • To practice faith, hope, and love in daily life
  • To create professional and conductive working atmosphere for educators and staff

Our School Logo

The logo contains multiple symbols and features each having their own meaning: Eagle bird: Symbolizes strength, perserverance and courage. This symbol embodies the character of our students, to be both independently driven yet globally concerned as well as people of integrity, competency, and faith Cross-shaped star: Cross symbolizes the sacrifice of Jesus Christ to save mankind from SIN. It also symbolizes victory over self-centeredness, which is not in harmony with the will of God. MCM XCIV: Represents the year when Santa Laurensia was first founded which was 1996

Our Curriculum

Edexcel International IGCSE Curriculum

We adopt the globally recognized Edexcel IGCSE framework to prepare students with strong academic foundations and internationally benchmarked standards.

CS Subject System

Our Core Subject system allows students to choose 3 subjects from the curriculum. It may be a mix of STEM and Social subjects, or it can be purely STEM or Social Subjects. It depends entirely on where our students see themselves in the future.

Elective Classes

Each year, students may choose 2 electives from a wide selection of arts and sports courses, including the option to focus on either or both disciplines.

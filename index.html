Welcome to Santa Laurensia

Empowering future leaders through excellence in holistic and bilingual education.

Santa Laurensia is a progressive Roman Catholic school founded in 1994. It offers a bilingual (English and Indonesian), holistic education for primary and secondary students. With a diverse team of local and international teachers, the school fosters global awareness and cultural appreciation. It emphasizes lifelong learning, personal growth, and empowers students to navigate future global challenges.

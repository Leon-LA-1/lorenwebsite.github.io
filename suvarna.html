Our Location

Our newest branch of the school is based in the newly opened Suvarna Sutera housing complex, which in itself is a branch of the Alam Sutera group. With this newest branch, we aim to further our reach so that students may receive quality education closer to home.

How to Get Here

The school is accessible by car, bus, and motorcycle. We're about 34 minutes our branch in the Alam Sutera region.

Brief History

Santa Laurensia School established its Suvarna Sutera campus in 2018 to extend its holistic, Catholic-based education to the growing communities in Tangerang. The primary school officially opened on July 12, 2018, followed by the senior high school on March 18, 2019. This expansion was driven by the school's mission to provide a nurturing and globally minded learning environment that fosters intellectual, emotional, and spiritual growth. Despite initial delays due to local concerns over the nature of the development, the project proceeded after clarifications confirmed it was an educational facility, not a religious institution. Today, the Suvarna Sutera campus offers Kindergarten through Senior High School programs, continuing Santa Laurensia's commitment to excellence in education.
